Volunteering trip to Malawi August 2019

Hi Everyone,

As some of you are aware, I am travelling to Malawi next summer for two weeks,
to build two houses, for two orphaned and vulnerable families. I will be travelling
as part of a "See, Serve, Speak" team.

My girlfriend Tash volunteered in Malawi with Habitat for Humanity last year
and I was so inspired by her account of the amazing work that’s being done,
that I decided to get on board for the summer 2019 trip. Habitat for Humanity
are working to help families locally here in Ireland
and in many countries across the world.
This focus upon local- global sustainable development is just one of the many
factors that contributed to my decision to volunteer with Habitat.
We will travel together, alongside a team of other volunteers to support
Habitat’s work in Malawi. The programme builds simple homes,
facilitates training on HIV and Malaria prevention, and encourages will writing.

Habitat for Humanity is making a huge difference to the lives of many people
around the world. Through sustainable development,

I need to raise a total of €2,900 before I go.
I will be making my own donation to cover flights/ other personal costs.
This means that any money raised through donations
will go towards the actual build or be fed into other Habitat projects.

To find out more, please feel free to explore this site.

Habitat for Humanity Ireland-


what do they do?

Habitat for Humanity Ireland was founded in 2002.
It is a non-profit organisation, working on behalf
of and in partnership with Habitat for Humanity
International. They are dedicated to eliminating and to
substandard housing worldwide adequate, affordable
shelter a matter of conscience and action.
